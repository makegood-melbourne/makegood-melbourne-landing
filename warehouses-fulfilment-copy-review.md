# Warehouses & Fulfilment Copy Review

## Overall Assessment

The pasted copy is strong and broadly suitable for implementation. It keeps the page focused on **warehouse, fulfilment, 3PL, e-commerce storage, distribution and dispatch facilities**, rather than drifting into trucking companies or heavy vehicle operations. The tone is practical, direct and consistent with the Make Good Melbourne voice.

The page is also a good fit for the next incremental website improvement because it creates a specific landing page for a homepage card that currently points to the broader `/industries/industrial/` page.

## Recommended Edits Before Implementation

| Location | Current wording | Recommended change | Reason |
|---|---|---|---|
| H1 | `WAREHOUSE & FULFILMENT CENTRE MAKE GOOD MELBOURNE` | Keep as-is | Clear, specific and search-aligned. |
| Hero copy | `Warehouse and fulfilment tenancies leave behind...` | Keep as-is | Strong opening and correctly frames fulfilment/logistics. |
| Section heading | `WAREHOUSE MAKE GOOD IS A DIFFERENT SCOPE` | Consider `WAREHOUSE MAKE GOOD IS A DIFFERENT KIND OF SCOPE` or keep as-is | Current heading is acceptable, but the alternative sounds slightly more natural. |
| Paragraph 2 | `the trades need to be coordinated` | Keep as-is | Practical and accurate. |
| Racking section | `without delay` | Consider `without holding up the next stage` | Slightly more contractor-like and less generic. |
| FAQ 1 | `without starting from a worse position than necessary` | Replace with `without creating extra work for the next stage` | Current phrase is understandable but a little awkward. |
| CTA | `We will review...` | Consider `We can review...` | Softer and more aligned with the site’s service tone. |
| CTA | `fixed-price quote` | Keep only if fixed-price quoting is definitely accurate for this scope | If some warehouse scopes require inspection or exclusions, this should be softened to `practical quote` or `clear quote`. |

## Implementation Recommendation

Proceed with this page as the first dedicated industry page, subject to the small wording refinements above. The implementation should add a new industry data object for the page, update the relevant homepage and service-page industry card links, keep `/industries/industrial/` live and connect this new page to the most relevant service pages in alphabetical order.
